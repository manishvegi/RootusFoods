The Story

The idea of bringing the best-known vegan food on your plate that is rarely noticed came when I was on a trip to the western ghats of India. While on this trip, we encountered a fruit very widely and widely produced, the Jackfruit. Although we grew up eating jackfruit during summers, we never realized it was so widely available and as we crossed the eastern state borders, we were surprised to discover it was so abundantly grown. However, it also left us in worry that only 8% of it was utilized. The fact that this fruit has multiple benefits excited us, but it also left us feeling sorrowful that we were not utilizing more than 12% of the fruit. That is when we thought about introducing this exotic, ancient fruit in a new form to your plate, thereby adding a new flavour to your meal with a lot of nutritional benefits.

The taste is neutral, so it goes well with all kinds of spices and flavors.
The meat substitute is not only promising in terms of Taste but also in terms of Nutrition, Physical and Sensory Properties With high fiber levels, no cholesterol, low fat, and low calories.
Naturally pest and disease resistant, jackfruit trees are drought resistant as well.
